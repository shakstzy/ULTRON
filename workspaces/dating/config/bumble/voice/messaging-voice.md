# messaging-voice (bumble)

Stub. Adithya fills this over time. Mirror tinder's `config/voice/messaging-voice.md` for the baseline tone, then adapt.

Bumble-specific: women send the first message OR set an Opening Move. Adithya's outbound is almost always a reply or an Opening-Move response, NOT a cold opener. Replies should:

- Pick up on something specific in what she said. Not generic acknowledgment.
- Stay under 3 sentences.
- One question at a time, max.
- No em dashes. No "how was your day."
- Move toward number/date by message #5 or #6.
