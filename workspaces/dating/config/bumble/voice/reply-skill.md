# reply-skill (bumble)

Stub. The reply playbook for Bumble matches.

Replies are ranked by:
1. Specificity (anchors on a concrete detail she said)
2. Forward motion (gives her something to react to)
3. Brevity (3 sentences max; ideally 1-2)
4. Voice match (no AI tells, no formal greetings)

Anti-patterns: "haha that's so cool", "tell me more", any question that could be sent verbatim to anyone.
