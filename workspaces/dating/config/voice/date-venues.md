## Selection Logic

When picking a venue for a proposed date:

1. Default to coffee unless she signaled otherwise (she mentioned tea, bubble tea, wanting to walk, dislike of coffee)
2. If she's in a different neighborhood than Adithya, pick the venue closest to her (not central)
3. If she's under 21, never default to a place that leads with alcohol
4. Match vibe tags to conversation energy -- if the thread has been playful, pick a lively spot; if it's been more reflective, pick a quieter one

## Day/Time Defaults

- Weekday: evenings (6–8 pm)
- Weekend: late morning (10 am – 12 pm) for coffee / walks
- Never propose after 9 pm for a first date
- Never propose before 9 am
