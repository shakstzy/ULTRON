## AI Notes

### Physical Robotics AI Data Opportunities

- Biggest buyers of data currently in physical robotics AI space
- Key data types in demand:

    - Multi-point images of objects
    - Disparity maps (depth maps from iPhone LiDAR sensors)
    - Audio from robotic hands (mechanical creaking for touch sensors)
    - Skeletal structure data (human bone positioning)
    - Camera recordings of robotic arm movements in lab settings
- Amazon buying 3D scans of household products from users
- Gaussian Splats technology - new 3D environment recreation from phone photos

    - More compute-efficient than traditional methods
    - Released 1-2 months ago

### Data Quality & User Reputation System

- Need tiered approach for different data quality levels:

    1. Human tier - general crowdsourced tasks
    2. Labs tier - certified accounts with higher quality assurance
- Reputation scoring system requirements:

    - Task completion count + accuracy rate (like IMDB rating system)
    - Specialization tracking by field (medical, legal, etc.)
    - Level progression to unlock higher-paying tasks
- Biometric verification considerations:

    - Face ID/fingerprint authentication for sessions
    - Video recording during audio tasks
    - Gyroscope data to verify real movement

### Audio Data Improvements

- Replace generic “rejected” feedback with specific reasons:

    - Highlight exact timestamp of issues (e.g., “0.23s dog barking detected”)
    - Background noise identification and labeling
    - Turn rejections into additional data points
- Director’s notes for emotional audio:

    - Replace vague prompts like “sound angry”
    - Provide scenario context: “You forgot keys leaving for funeral”
- Secondary speaker demand identified:

    - Non-native speakers (e.g., Spanish native speaking English)
    - Haitian Creole and Arabic Gulf specifically mentioned
    - Require native language collection in user profiles

### Competitive Intelligence & Market Gaps

- Established players have self-reinforcing data loops:

    - Deepgram powers Granola, Vapi, others
    - Customer data flows back to improve models
    - Difficult to break into these cycles
- Defense against platform expansion:

    - DoorDash, Airbnb, Fiverr could enter data collection
    - Niantic gaming company precedent
    - Need agent-native positioning as primary moat
- Model deficiency research approach:

    - Run existing benchmarks to identify worst performers
    - A/B test human vs. model review of submissions
    - Target labs with “your model sucks, we can help” sales approach

### Immediate App Development Priorities

- Rejection reason display with audio highlighting
- Example task completion before real task access
- Optional additional data collection fields:

    - Location data per recording
    - Video capture during audio recording
    - Increased payout for more data provided
- Multi-channel conversational audio stitching:

    - Script portion + impromptu response combination
    - Service call recordings, coffee orders, daily life audio

### Research & Outreach Tasks

- Compile comprehensive list of data buyers (less than 100 companies)
- Benchmark analysis of model deficiencies by vendor
- LinkedIn outreach for disparity map/household item data buyers
- Secondary speaker task design and implementation
- Fiverr/Upwork auto-posting MVP for task arbitrage

### Next Steps

- Cemal: Research model benchmarks and deficiency identification
- Adithya: Compile data buyer universe list, continue demand-side research
- Daniel: Secondary speaker task design, native language requirement implementation
- Team: Design biometric verification flow, rejection reason display system
