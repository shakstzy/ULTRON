## AI Notes

### Product Concept - AI Agent Evaluation Platform

- Building “assurance layer” for AI agents in regulated workflows

    - Traditional evals are “vibe checks” - LLMs grading other LLMs with bias
    - Need deterministic evaluation as agents handle money movement, filing reports, customer data
- Core solution: Verticalized gyms (simulated work environments)

    - Agents train on specialized scenarios vs generic prompting
    - Deterministic oracles (compilers, solvers, system checks) provide scoring
    - Multi-model tribunal system for subjective evaluations
    - Produces counterexample traces showing exactly where/why agents fail
- Competitive differentiation from existing players:

    - Observability platforms (LangSmith, BrainTrust): Good tracing but collapse into LLM-as-judge
    - Automated eval companies (Patronus AI, Galileo): Strong scoring but weak on action verification
    - Data labeling (Scale AI): Expensive human throughput, doesn’t scale with cost

### Go-to-Market Strategy

- Key advisors secured:

    - Head of AI at Story (neighbor, UT PhD with frontier lab connections)
    - CPO of CoreWeave (direct collaboration through Dino Say Network role)
- First design partner: BlackRock Aladdin team

    - College friend runs their current evals
    - Will pay them initially for data access
    - Eventually license verticalized judge model back to them
- Validation feedback from Anti Metal (AWS auto-deployment):

    - Confirmed generic evals inadequate for specialized use cases
    - No existing framework for building verticalized judge models
    - Need deep product integration - can’t just sell data externally
- Pricing model: API-based licensing of verticalized judge models
