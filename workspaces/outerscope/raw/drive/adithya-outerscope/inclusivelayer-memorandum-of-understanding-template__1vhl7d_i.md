MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

This Memorandum of Understanding (“MOU”) is entered into by and between InclusiveLayer (Cayman) Ltd., having its principal place of business at \[ADDRESS\], Cayman Islands (“InclusiveLayer”), and \[Partner\].

1\. **Objective**

1.1 **The Primary Objective.** The objective of this MOU is to express a willingness between both parties to pursue a collaboration in which InclusiveLayer will provide high-frequency, DeFi-focused infrastructure services to Partner. This partnership aims to leverage InclusiveLayer’s capabilities as a specialized infrastructure chain to support and enhance Partner’s operations, as well as outline the preliminary terms and conditions under which such collaboration would potentially take place.

1.2 **Non-binding.** The parties hereto agree and acknowledge that any addendum to this MOU does not constitute a legally binding agreement and that the general legal provisions set forth in the body of this MOU shall be the only binding provisions.  

2\. **General Terms of the MOU**

2.1 **Duration of the MOU.** Given this MOU is merely an indication of interest and not a legally binding agreement, either party may terminate this MOU at any time, provided that the confidentiality and governing law provisions set forth herein shall survive any such termination.  Further, this MOU and all provisions set forth herein (including confidentiality and governing law) shall be terminated and superseded upon the execution of a legally binding agreement between the parties.

2.2 **Coordination.** In order to carry out and fulfill the aims of this MOU, each party will appoint an appropriate person(s) to represent its organization and to coordinate and work in earnest towards a legally binding collaboration. Such person(s) appointed by \[Partner\] and InclusiveLayer will meet periodically to discuss progress and plan activities. 

2.3 **Technical and Financial Support.** Addendums to this MOU may be entered into to further outline additional activities and frameworks for the intended longer term collaboration. These Addendums will provide a detailed description of the intended role, responsibility and financial contribution of each party. Work plans and any specific commitment will be clearly outlined in the Addendums, provided, however, such work plans and commitments shall be non-binding until a legally binding agreement is signed by the relevant parties. 

2.4 **Confidentiality.** Each party agrees that it shall not, except where explicitly agreed to in writing by both parties disclose any Confidential Information in relation to this partnership or the business affairs or method of carrying on the business of the other, without written consent of both parties. “Confidential Information” means the terms of the MOU (including but not limited to the existence of this MOU) and all information concerning the parties or any party to which another party is provided access by the virtue of its activities as a result of this MOU. Confidential Information does not include (a) information that has been publicly disclosed in writing by the disclosing party, (b) information that the receiving party can reasonably demonstrate was in its possession prior to disclosure by the disclosing party, or (c) information that was rightfully received by a party from a third party without restriction or duty of confidentiality. Confidential Information acquired hereunder by either party will be treated as proprietary information of the disclosing party and shall not be used for any purpose other than the collaboration or disclosed to any third party, except such Party’s professional advisors, consultants or its employees who have a reasonable need to know such information, it being understood that any violation of this clause by such person shall be treated as though such party had violated this clause itself. 

2.5 **Communication.** All notices and other communication under this MOU in connection herewith shall be written in English language and shall be sent to an email of the appointed contact person. Any notice shall be effective from the date on which it is sent to the correctly designated email. 

2.6 **Addendum.** Any Addendum to this MOU shall be in writing and signed by both parties and the terms and provisions of this MOU shall apply to such Addendums. 

2.7 **Insurance.** It is the responsibility of each entity to insure themselves against any potential liability associated with this MOU and neither party shall bear any responsibility for any liabilities that arise for the other party by virtue of entering into or performing activities under this MOU. 

2.8 **Jurisdiction, Governing Law.** This MOU shall be governed by and construed under the laws of the Cayman Islands. The parties hereto agree that the courts of the Cayman Islands have jurisdiction to hear and determine any action, suit or proceeding, and settle any disputes, in connection with this MOU and accordingly submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Cayman Islands courts.

2.9 **Assignment.** Neither party shall assign this Agreement, in whole or in part, without the prior written consent of the other party. 

3\. **Other Provisions**

3.1 Each party acknowledges and agrees that this MOU permits either party to promote and communicate this partnership publicly in good faith in a manner that positively reflects on both parties. Each party will share logos and images they permit the other party to use, and stipulate any specific language they would like the other party to employ in their communications about the project and partnership. 

3.2 \[Partner\] shall immediately inform InclusiveLayer of any event, which could have a negative impact on the reputation of InclusiveLayer. Likewise, InclusiveLayer shall immediately inform \[Partner\] of any event, which could have a negative impact on the reputation of \[Partner\]. 

3.3 \[Partner\] shall not use the name of InclusiveLayer In any manner deemed controversial, offensive, inaccurate, or in any other manner that reflects poorly on InclusiveLayer. Likewise, InclusiveLayer shall not use the name of \[Partner\] in any manner deemed controversial, offensive, inaccurate, or in any other manner that reflects poorly on \[Partner\]. 

3.4 Each party shall cover the expenses of its staff and any fees associated with the participation of its staff (ex: transportation, communications, lodging, etc.) in the support of this activity, unless otherwise agreed upon in writing. 

3.5 Each party is an independent party in this MOU, and no compensation or benefits are owed in either direction unless stipulated in another written agreement. 

3.6 If a court or other body of competent jurisdiction finds, or the parties mutually believe, any provision of this Agreement, or portion thereof, to be invalid or unenforceable, such provision will be enforced to the maximum extent permissible so as to effect the intent of the Parties, and the remainder of this MOU will continue in full force and effect.

3.7 In the event that the preceding conditions and Addendum are met, the parties shall work in good faith towards negotiating a definitive contract containing typical legal clauses, guarantees, declarations, indemnities, and obligations of the parties.

Non-Binding MOU Addendum

The Parties in the Collaboration aim to achieve:

* Enhanced Scalability, Security, and Reliability: Deliver robust, scalable, and secure solutions for decentralized applications and services by leveraging Inclusive Layer’s vertically integrated SDK and high-performance blockchain infrastructure.  
* Accelerated Innovation: Combine Inclusive Layer’s expertise in multi-chain abstraction, liquidity aggregation, and seamless account management with the Partner’s operational strengths to drive innovation in decentralized consumer applications.

The Collaboration shall have the following technical roles and responsibilities:

* InclusiveLayer:  
  * Provide comprehensive technical support, modular SDKs, and detailed documentation to facilitate seamless integration with Inclusive Layer’s infrastructure.  
  * Ensure developers have access to tools for efficient account management, liquidity aggregation, and chain abstraction.  
  * Offer specialized knowledge in multi-chain operations, cross-chain liquidity optimization, and wallet integrations to enhance the Partner’s service capabilities.  
  * Simplify blockchain complexities for the Partner through Inclusive Layer’s pre-built infrastructure.  
  * Ensure smooth deployment and operation of decentralized solutions using Inclusive Layer’s tools designed for consumer-focused use cases.  
  * Optimize performance for high-frequency applications requiring low latency and high throughput.  
  * Continuously enhance Inclusive Layer’s infrastructure to meet evolving demands for decentralized applications across EVM and non-EVM chains.  
  * Regularly update SDKs and tools to provide cutting-edge solutions tailored to user needs.

* \[Partner\]:  
  * Operate services or applications integrated with Inclusive Layer’s blockchain infrastructure while ensuring high availability, uptime, and performance in line with industry standards  
  * Provide actionable feedback on the functionality, reliability, and security of deployed solutions to help Inclusive Layer enhance its SDKs and infrastructure  
  * Work with Inclusive Layer to identify opportunities for innovation within their ecosystem and co-develop strategies to drive adoption of decentralized technologies  
  * Leverage Inclusive Layer’s tools to deliver exceptional user experiences while maintaining operational excellence in service delivery

The Collaboration shall have the following publicity roles and responsibilities:

* The Parties will issue mutually agreeable partnership announcements on representative social media channels  
* The Parties will make available to each other, upon request, promotional materials relating to the Project, including but not limited to stills, logos (collectively, the “Project Materials”) in marketing and brand preferred formats  
* Counterparties will be granted moniker of “Official Partner”  
* The Parties will make a mutually agreed upon public announcement or press release with respect to the Collaboration, and will cooperate in good faith and mutually agreed upon marketing initiatives, including but not limited to content review and approval, launch calendar and planning, and content amplifications  
* The Parties grant to each other a worldwide, revocable, limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-sublicensable right to use each other’s mark and Project Materials, for the purpose of marketing the Collaboration. Each Party shall use each other’s marks in accordance with the other Party’s trademark usage guidelines and quality control standards.
