## Pattern

- Tone: sharp, slang-heavy, tightly inside-joked. "Wsg nigs," "Ur in ur Jackson heights phase huh," "This Zach is zaching."
- Geographic split: a New York contingent (with `-nyc` suffix) and a residual Bay Area / Berkeley contingent.
- 1:1 traffic is light; group chats carry the volume.
