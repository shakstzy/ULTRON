# Grad Group (Berkeley Core)

A seven-person group formed around UC Berkeley graduation in May 2024. Core members are the Berkeley cohort: Saif, Prabhat, Aamir, Rushil, Maneesh, and Adithya. The group formed to coordinate a graduation photo shoot.

## Notable thread

### May 2024 — graduation coordination and jersey beef

The only shard covers finals week 2024. Saif opens with: "Layer N merch is banned from pics" — a joke about not being caught in a startup's gear in graduation photos. Adithya fires back: "Im sliding w that netapp shit on frfr" (referencing a NetApp internship). Saif: "collecting companies like infinity stones." This is peak cohort energy.

The graduation photo planning devolves into chaotic scheduling — Saif's exam week leaves almost no free slots. Aamir tries to corral everyone. "CHEWMEER" from Saif, unprompted.
