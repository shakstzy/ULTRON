The signature thread of this group. Pseuhas has to physically restrain a roommate (referred to as "this guy" / "kid") from doing nitrous oxide. Documented in cinematic step-by-step:

The roommate had been out clubbing the night before, came home at 6am, "Claimed he woke up next to a canny." Pseuhas offered "A) imma text your mom or B) let me empty the canny." Kid chose A. Pseuhas chose B for him. Saif's comment: "Being addicted to whippits actually might be the most degenerate thing I've ever heard."
