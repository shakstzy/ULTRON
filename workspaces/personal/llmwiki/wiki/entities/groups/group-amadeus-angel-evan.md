## Notable thread

### January 2025 — LA wildfire threat

Evan opens at 1am: "Yall we might need to evacuate." Adithya wakes up and checks in. Amadeus has already gone to his mom's house with his important stuff. Angel is at family nearby with evacuation zones creeping toward Klein Ct.

By evening, Amadeus: "Yeah it's 10% contained rn." Evan: "To be fair it's happening way worse in boujie ass pacific palisades lol." Relief.

Then 11pm, a new evacuation map drops. Amadeus shares it. Multiple react in panic. Adithya: "Thank god we're on the opposite side of the highway bruh / Or else house would be cooked." He cancels his flight home. Amadeus: "Honestly yeah bro just wait for shi to die down." Reports of 2+ week fire burn. Amadeus: "Oh nah 2 weeks imma just kms."
