# Group: Dad + Mom + Adithya

Three-way thread between [[wiki/entities/people/mom]], [[wiki/entities/people/dad]], and Adithya. The thread carries the load-bearing emotional content of the family relationship that does NOT appear in the 1:1 dad thread. Mom uses it to narrate events to dad in writing.

## Recent state (April 2026)

April 29 is the cleanest snapshot of the dynamic. Mom:

> "Despite me telling him to not go, he went to gym. He said he will come back on time. Which he didn't... It is easy to blame all the problems on me and harass me verbally, emotionally and physically. It is hard to self reflect."

Then a doctor visit:

> "They found a warm and painful cyst on the left inside of my vagina. They have given me a ob Gyn referral."

Dad's contributions are short and operational ("I have the Lexus. He has taken the van"). At the end of the same thread, after a separate update on Adithya's nose bleed:

> Dad: "Was the blood which was blocking his breathing. I feel terrible I caused it. Anger turns us into animals."

This implies a physical altercation between Dad and Adithya around late April 2026 with a nose injury. Worth pulling forward as a discrete event in [[wiki/concepts/family-tension]].

## Notable

- Pattern: mom narrates Adithya's behavior to dad in writing, often while Adithya is on the thread reading it. Form of triangulation.
- Cyst (mom) and nose injury (Adithya, dad-caused) are both health events from late April 2026 worth tracking.
