# Klein Roommates

Roommate / tenant group at 13245 Klein Ct, Adithya's Austin property. Multiple variants of the group exist as members rotated through. Mix of formal tenants (paying rent to Adithya) and friend-tier roommates (Evan, Amadeus).

## Notable threads

### November 2024 — appliance saga

The dishwasher, washing machine, and dryer all break in a tight window. Evan handles technician scheduling. The technician fixes the washer (broken door seal), parts ordered for fuller repair. Evan also flags general filthiness:

> Evan, 2024-11-08: "my gf who is only here once or twice a week was so grossed out by the condition of the counters and tables that she cleaned it. That's really not right... For the last month please keep the place clean. It's just a month."

> Evan, 2024-11-09: "Yall it's been less than 24 fucking hours. Why is the counter filthy again? My girlfriend isn't your fucking maid. Clean up"

References "Bryson" and "Joko" as roommates from this era. Bryson left enough of an impression to become "the ghost of Bryson" in January 2026.

> me, 2026-01-17: "Yo can everyone take care of your dishes by tonight, ive taken care of my dishes asw theres literally mold forming underneath the dishes atp"
