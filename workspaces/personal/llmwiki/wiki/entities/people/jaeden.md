# Jaeden

Eclipse Labs business-development counterpart. Owns a meditation app called **Selfpause** with 150K users who record personal affirmations. Aggregating podcasts and personal-audio data for Eclipse to license.

## Recent state (April 2026)

April 7: Jaeden proposes a clean licensing structure — sign one master agreement with his podcast studio, then his studio handles per-podcast licensing downstream. Eclipse sends Jaeden a template.

Reference deal terms (the "60/10 split" Adithya described to [[wiki/entities/people/sydney-huang]] in the same window):

- $25/hour audio rate.
- First batch: 7.58 hours of WAV samples = $189.50.
- Eclipse ops email: `ops@eclipse.builders`.
- Jaeden payout: PayPal `jaeden@fiund.com` or ACH/wire to his business.

April 9: Jaeden surfaces the Selfpause angle:

> "It just occurred to me that I own a personal meditation app with 150k users who record personal affirmations for themselves. In our Terms it says we can use their data for training AI. Would that data set be interesting to you guys assuming we clean it and enhance all the audio so it sounds HQ?"

April 14: scope expands. "Were you saying I have a total of 455 hours of audio?" Jaeden checks Selfpause terms with his legal team for compliance before committing.
