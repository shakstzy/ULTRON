# Johny Hadar

Producer in the Hadar's LA circle. Met Adithya at Hadar's place in early 2024.

## Recent state (March 2024)

Day after they met, Johny reaches out and Adithya thanks him, asking for loops. Johny immediately offers to connect him to a producer on his roster who "was just on Jimin's last single" and is "pretty saucy" with loops and samples.

The next day Johny adds another connection: Dylan Hyde, described as "constantly making things."

Adithya accepts both introductions.
