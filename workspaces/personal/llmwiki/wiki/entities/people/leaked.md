# Leaked (Service)

Fan-messaging and music distribution service at leaked.fm. Adithya uses it to drop audio snippets (leaks) to tiered listener lists.

## Recent state (May 2024)

Two leaks sent via SMS bot interface:

- May 2, 2024: "Oxycotin" (WAV, 24MB) sent to C-list (4 listeners). Message: "1/0"
- May 16, 2024: "Bengal" sent to C-list (5 listeners). Message: "S tier musix"

The C-list had grown from 4 to 5 listeners between sends.

## Notable

- Service works entirely via iMessage: text to confirm tier, title, message, then YES to send.
- Listener tiers: A-list, B-list, C-list.
- Both tracks are otherwise unreleased titles in the corpus: "Oxycotin" and "Bengal."
