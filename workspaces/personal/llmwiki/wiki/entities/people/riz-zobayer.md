# Riz

Uncle of Zobayer, a tenant at 13245 Klein Ct. Reached out separately to monitor the lease process and serve as a family liaison.

## The arc (September 2025)

Riz introduced himself as Zobayer's uncle and asked for a breakdown of the current tenants, framing it as wanting to "ensure the leasing process goes smoothly from both sides." Phone tag at first, then connected. He flagged Adithya's voicemail wasn't set up.

The thread then shifted from vetting to managing an already-live situation: Adithya forwarded communication with Zobayer to Riz directly, including screenshots. Riz's response made clear he understood the stakes:

> "Thank you. I appreciate it. Yeah, I hope all goes well. I know how painful it is with CA law and eviction."

In late September Adithya shared a proposed payment arrangement, noting his dad wasn't going below $1,050 total. On September 30, Adithya sent a video of a cake cutting for Zobayer's birthday; Riz reacted with love.

## Notable

- Riz is the adult family guarantor figure -- reached out proactively, not just reactively.
