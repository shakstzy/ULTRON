### **Introduction to Inclusive Layer \- The Consumer Chain**

#### **Empowering Blockchain Developers**

Our protocol offers a powerful suite of **SDKs** to simplify blockchain development for consumer applications. Whether you’re scaling an existing dApp or building from scratch, Inclusive Layer eliminates the complexities of managing fragmented blockchain ecosystems, multi-wallet integrations, and cross-chain liquidity. With us, developers can focus on creating exceptional consumer experiences while we handle the heavy lifting of blockchain infrastructure.

**Core Features**

1. **Account Abstraction**  
   * One account, Unified balance, for all chains—allows your consumers to manage wallets across **MetaMask, Phantom, Kepler, TON**, and more, all from a single interface.  
   * Enable gasless transactions and seamless onboarding.  
2. **Unified Liquidity \- Intent-Based RFQ Architecture**  
   * Access liquidity with best prices from top DEXs, aggregators, and market makers like **GSR** and **Manifold**.  
   * Guaranteed pricing through RFQ-based transactions.  
   * Ensure real-time quotes with minimal latency.  
3. **Chain Abstraction**  
   * Simplify connectivity across EVM, non-EVM, and L2 ecosystems.  
     1. Currently supporting Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Polygon, Solana, Bitcoin L2  
   * Build cross-chain applications without managing complex blockchain infrastructure.

### **Use Cases**

Inclusive Layer powers a wide range of applications:

* **DeFi Platforms:** DEXs, derivatives, lending, insurance, memecoin   
* **Meme Coins and Launchpads:** Allow users to buy and sell meme coins using any token on any chain  
* **AI Agents and Launchpads**: Create cross-chain avenues for minting, trading, and LPing for AI agents.  
* **Gaming:** Enable seamless bridging of in-game assets and tokens.  
* **Decentralized Identity Providers (DID):** Simplify cross-chain authentication.  
* **DePIN Networks:** Create decentralized infrastructure with tokenized assets.

---

### **Why Inclusive Layer?**

* **Cost-Effective:** No audits, no specialized blockchain developers, and no integrations necessary.   
* **Remove Blockchain Code:** No prerequisite for blockchain developers. Code with the framework you’re most comfortable with.   
* **Time Is Money:** All blockchain and partner integrations are handled by us, allowing you to go to market quicker without the need for finding market makers, bridges, or liquidity providers  
* **Chain-Agnostic Framework:** Operate seamlessly across EVM and non-EVM ecosystems.
