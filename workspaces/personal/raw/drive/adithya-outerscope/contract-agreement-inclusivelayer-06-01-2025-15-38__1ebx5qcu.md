CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Consulting Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is made as of Jan 13th, by and
between Outerscope, a company incorporated under the laws of the United States, with its
principal place of business at 5509 Casco Walk, Austin, TX (the "Company") &
InclusiveLayer (the "Client").

1. Consulting Relationship
During the Term (as defined below), Outerscope will provide consulting services to
InclusiveLayer as described herein and in Exhibit A attached hereto (the "Services"). The
relationship between Outerscope and InclusiveLayer will be that of an independent
contractor.

2. Services
Outerscope agrees to perform the following services for InclusiveLayer:

- Develop and refine InclusiveLayer' business development funnel & support

go-to-market activities.

- Develop an end-to-end tokenomics strategy
- Develop partnerships and Memorandums of Understanding (MOUs) with projects in

InclusiveLayer' go-to-market strategy, such as token creators & listers, and
ecosystem deals with product launch partners.

- Develop incentivization strategies to maximize market traction in each key area.
- Attend key industry events to drive business development results.

3. Fees, Expenses and Non-Cash Compensation
Fees: InclusiveLayer shall pay Outerscope a monthly retainer of $7,500. This arrangement is
subject to a three-month commitment from both parties.

Expenses: Outerscope is responsible for all expenses incurred while performing the
Services unless otherwise agreed upon by InclusiveLayer.

4. Term and Termination
Term: This Agreement will commence on the Effective Date (Jan 13th) and continue for three
months (the "Initial Term"). It shall automatically renew for additional one-year terms (each a
"Renewal Term") unless either party provides 30 days' prior written notice of non-renewal.

Termination: Either party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice within five
business days after receiving notice.

5. Confidentiality
Outerscope agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information received from
InclusiveLayer during the course of this Agreement. Confidential information includes any
data marked as confidential or that should reasonably be understood as confidential.

6. Indemnification
Outerscope shall indemnify and hold harmless InclusiveLayer from any claims arising from
negligent acts or omissions by Outerscope or its agents in connection with this Agreement.

7. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the
United States.

9. Miscellaneous
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding its subject
matter and supersedes all prior agreements. Any modifications must be made in writing and
signed by both parties.
