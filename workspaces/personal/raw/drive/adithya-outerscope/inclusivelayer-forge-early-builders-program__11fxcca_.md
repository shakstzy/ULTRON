FORGE: EARLY BUILDERS PROGRAM

**Overview**

The InclusiveLayer Early Builders Program is an initiative designed to support and empower a select group of innovators deploying solutions on InclusiveLayer’s high-performance, DeFi-focused blockchain infrastructure. This program aims to drive groundbreaking innovation by providing tailored resources, technical support through the InclusiveLayer SDK, and unique opportunities for early adopters who are shaping the future of decentralized finance and blockchain technology with InclusiveLayer.

**Program Benefits**

Builders accepted into the Early Builders Program will enjoy the following benefits:

1. **Privileged Co-Marketing Opportunities**  
   * Early Builders will collaborate with InclusiveLayer on co-marketing initiatives to amplify their projects.  
   * Builders will be granted the prestigious title of “Early Builder” and receive endorsements from InclusiveLayer, including visibility on InclusiveLayer’s official platforms.

2. **Endorsed Builder Recognition**  
   * Early Builders will have a dedicated spot on a special page on the InclusiveLayer website. This page will showcase their projects, providing exposure to the broader blockchain and DeFi communities as well as potential collaborators.  
   * The endorsement signifies that these builders are trusted partners working closely with InclusiveLayer.

3. **Access to Early Resources**  
   * Early Builders will gain access to InclusiveLayer’s work-in-progress documentation, offering insights into cutting-edge developments and technical guidance for integrating solutions with InclusiveLayer’s infrastructure.  
   * Builders will have direct communication channels with the InclusiveLayer team for technical support, collaboration, and feedback.

4. **Early Access to Full-Functionality Features**  
   * Participants will have access to InclusiveLayer’s early definitive infrastructure features, including the latest tools in the InclusiveLayer SDK, offering full functionality ahead of general availability.

**Eligibility Criteria**

The Early Builders Program is open to a limited number of builders who meet the following criteria:

* Active Development on InclusiveLayer Infrastructure: Applicants must be actively developing or planning to deploy solutions on InclusiveLayer’s high-performance, DeFi-focused blockchain infrastructure, leveraging tools such as the InclusiveLayer SDK and its EVM compatibility.  
* Commitment to Innovation and Alignment with InclusiveLayer’s Mission: Builders should demonstrate a strong commitment to driving innovation in decentralized finance (DeFi) or blockchain technology. Their projects should align with InclusiveLayer’s mission of advancing decentralized infrastructure through scalable, high-frequency capabilities.  
* Collaboration and Feedback: Participants must be willing to collaborate closely with the InclusiveLayer team, providing valuable feedback on the platform’s functionality, usability, and performance to help refine and enhance its ecosystem.

**Program Expectations**

As part of the Early Builders Program, participants are expected to:

* Provide Feedback: Actively engage with InclusiveLayer by offering detailed feedback on the infrastructure’s performance, usability, and areas for potential improvement. This collaboration helps refine and enhance InclusiveLayer’s blockchain ecosystem.  
* Participate in Co-Marketing Activities: Collaborate with InclusiveLayer on co-marketing efforts, such as case studies, blog posts, joint announcements, or webinars, to showcase their projects and highlight the benefits of building on the InclusiveLayer platform.  
* Ensure Best Practices: Maintain the security, reliability, and functionality of their deployments in accordance with industry best practices for decentralized systems, ensuring alignment with InclusiveLayer’s high-performance standards.
