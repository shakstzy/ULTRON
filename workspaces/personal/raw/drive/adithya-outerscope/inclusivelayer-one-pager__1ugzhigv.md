# Inclusive Layer: A One-Pager Introduction

### Overview

Inclusive Layer is a cutting-edge, vertically integrated Software Development Kit (SDK) designed to simplify blockchain development for builders. It eliminates the complexities of managing fragmented blockchain infrastructure, multi-wallet integrations, and cross-chain liquidity. With Inclusive Layer, developers can focus on creating exceptional consumer experiences while we handle the heavy lifting of blockchain infrastructure.

## Unique Features

### Comprehensive SDK Options

Inclusive Layer offers three tailored SDKs to suit diverse development needs:  
\- Full Suite SDK: Combines account/wallet management, chain abstraction, and liquidity aggregation.  
\- Chain Abstraction & Account Management SDK: Simplifies multi-chain operations without requiring specialized blockchain expertise.  
\- Account Management & Liquidity Aggregation SDK: Ensures seamless wallet integration and access to optimal liquidity rates.

### Cross-Chain Account Management

\- Manage accounts across wallets like MetaMask, Phantom, Kepler, and Ton Wallet.  
\- Unified interface for EVM and non-EVM chains, removing the need for separate integrations.

### Liquidity Aggregation

\- Aggregate liquidity from top providers such as GSR and Manifold.  
\- Access quotes from leading DEXs with minimal latency and the best rates.

### Chain Abstraction

\- Unlock access to multiple EVM and non-EVM chains with native assets.  
\- No need to hire Solidity or Rust developers or manage complex cross-chain infrastructure.

## Benefits

### Simplified Development

\- Focus entirely on user experience (UX) without worrying about blockchain infrastructure.  
\- Reduce time-to-market by leveraging pre-built tools and integrations.

### Incentive Programs

\- Reward users with loyalty points, token airdrops, and yields from DeFi products.  
\- Build user retention through transaction-based reward systems.

### Data Tools for Informed Decisions

\- Access insights into user behavior, token demand trends, and liquidity metrics across chains.  
\- Optimize application performance and business strategy using actionable data.

### Cost Efficiency

\- Avoid high costs associated with hiring specialized developers or building custom infrastructure.  
\- Leverage Inclusive Layer’s ready-to-use tools to reduce operational expenses.

## Use Cases

Inclusive Layer is ideal for building diverse decentralized applications (dApps), including:  
\- Trading platforms (options, futures, exchanges)  
\- Social networks and messengers  
\- Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks (DePIN)  
\- Memecoin platforms and creator communities

## Why Choose Inclusive Layer?

Inclusive Layer empowers builders with a unified solution that simplifies blockchain development while enhancing scalability, efficiency, and user engagement. By abstracting away the complexities of multi-chain operations, liquidity management, and wallet integrations, we enable developers to innovate faster and smarter.
