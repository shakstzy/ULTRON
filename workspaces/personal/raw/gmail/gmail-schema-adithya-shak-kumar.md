## 5. Content-based heuristics

- **If** sender is a real human's email (firstname@personaldomain or firstname.lastname@company.com) AND there's no "no-reply" / "noreply" / "donotreply" in the address, **then** treat as critical and ingest, **because** real humans are why this account exists; even cold outreach is briefly worth a glance.

- **If** subject starts with `Re: ` AND prior message in thread is from a human (not noreply), **then** real conversation worth preserving, **because** noreply senders never get replies.
- **If** sender is `*@convo.zillow.com` with randomized prefix, **then** Real-Estate-Mgmt critical (Klein Ct rental inquiry), **because** active rental income flow.
- **If** sender is `*@docusign.net` AND subject mentions "Mercury Insurance Group", **then** Klein property doc, **because** the Klein label tracks Mercury Condo policy correspondence.
- **If** subject contains `1098` or `Form 1098`, **then** tax-critical, **because** mortgage-interest tax forms.
- **If** body length is < 50 words AND sender is no-reply, **then** likely transactional one-shot (login code, receipt), **because** real humans write more.
- **If** subject contains digits-only or 5-7 character verification code, **then** one-time login, **because** these are MFA tokens.
- **If** message is in a thread with > 5 replies AND > 2 distinct human senders, **then** real ongoing conversation, **because** automation rarely creates deep multi-party threads.
- **If** thread has only 1 message AND sender is on the noreply allowlist (Drive comments, Calendly invites), **then** safe to trash automatically — only if no reply from `adithya.shak.kumar@gmail.com` exists, **because** the user's sent-mail set defines "engaged".
- **If** attachment is `.pdf` from a financial/insurance/medical sender, **then** preserve, **because** it's likely a statement/policy/result.
- **If** attachment is `.ics` (calendar invite), **then** treat the parent message as Calendar-Invites; if no human reply, archive/trash.
- **If** attachment is `.eml` or `.msg`, **then** forwarded mail — preserve and inherit handling from inner sender.
- **If** message body contains markup like `display: none` or hidden white-on-white text, **then** likely marketing tracking — apply Promo or trash.
- **If** the user (`adithya.shak.kumar@gmail.com`) is on `Bcc`, **then** they're cc'd-by-archive-rule rather than addressed — usually transactional copies, lower priority.
- **If** TO field contains > 10 recipients AND no human reply from Adithya, **then** broadcast newsletter — Newsletter-* family.
