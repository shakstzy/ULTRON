### What a16z Is Actually Evaluating

- Can you place a company in a competitive landscape using network + knowledge?
- Can you go technically deeper on due diligence than a generalist?
- Can you plug into a structured workflow?

    - Process: deal intake → CRM → analysis → writing → investment committee (sometimes 3 rounds) → written memo
- Early-stage lens is team-market fit, not MRR

    - Soft signal: can you imagine this founder on the GTC stage running a $100B company?
    - Hard signal: esoteric domain knowledge — do they know five levels deeper than the obvious?

- Pause before answering — plan a mental outline first

- Speak in paragraphs, not streams — avoid the tendency to ramble for 2–3 minutes per point
- Reference his specific investment memos to contextualize your answers to what he already believes
