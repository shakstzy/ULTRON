## AI Notes

### Product Vision Alignment

- Verifiable trust and governance layer for production AI
- Two core product areas:

    1. Managed service for trusted execution environments (TEEs)

          - Multi-cloud, vendor-agnostic confidential computing
          - Targets foundational model builders with capacity across 5+ providers
          - Differentiates from AWS Nitro Enclaves (complex, single-cloud)
    2. AI asset lineage and provenance tracking

          - Data/model/agent correlation during training
          - Addresses ML Ops tool limitations with IAM-bound controls
          - Pinterest and Adobe building internal solutions (validation of market need)
- Evolution from original Glass marketplace concept to managed software solution on existing customer infrastructure

### Target Customer Profiles

- Foundational model builders with proprietary models (not open-source)

    - Large providers like Anthropic scaling across heterogeneous infrastructure
    - Sensitivity to deployment locations and IP protection
- Enterprises and startups customizing open-source models

    - Taking base models, training on proprietary data
    - Need governance once models embed enterprise value
- Compute providers (lower priority)

    - Neo Cloud, CoreWeave, Nebius - reputation/security challenges
    - Partner channel opportunity after establishing end-customer demand

### Outreach Strategy

- Focus on scaling startups with notable brand recognition

    - Primary targets: Lovable, Cursor, Windsurf
    - Theory: Fear being relegated as wrappers around foundation models
    - Cursor investing in own models to reduce Claude dependency
- Target personas:

    1. Early-stage: CEO/Founder
    2. Series B+: Head of AI/ML platforms, Head of Engineering, CISO
- LinkedIn outreach via Chetan’s profile

    - Top 50 prospects initially (quality over quantity)
    - Connection + message approach
    - Goal: 5-6 calls scheduled over next 2 weeks
