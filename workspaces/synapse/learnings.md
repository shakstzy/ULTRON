## Operational rules

- Investor / accelerator / partner / vendor entities resolve through `schema.md` (`person`, `company`, `program`, `deal`).
- Cross-workspace people (e.g., a VC who also funds eclipse) point to `_global/entities/people/<slug>.md`. Synapse-specific notes stay in the synapse workspace.
