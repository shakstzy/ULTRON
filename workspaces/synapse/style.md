# Synapse Style

## Capitalization

Company / product names cap as their owners write them: Synapse, Y Combinator, Alliance, HF0, NVIDIA Inception, Google for Startups Cloud, Companyon, Redbeard, Denarii Labs.

## Numbers

- Dollar amounts: `$5M`, `$250k`, `$1.2B` (no spaces, abbreviated for round / valuations).
- Equity / dilution: `5%`, `0.25%` (no decimal padding beyond what's meaningful).
- Dates: ISO `YYYY-MM-DD` in frontmatter; `May 1, 2026` in body prose.
- Round names: `pre-seed`, `seed`, `Series A` (lowercase letter rounds, capital A onward).

## Frontmatter

- All wiki pages have YAML frontmatter as defined per type in `schema.md`.
- `last_touched` is updated by the wiki agent on every meaningful update.

## Tables

- Programs / fundraises tracked as tables sorted by recency, `status` column required.
- Use `—` (em dash) only in body prose, never in slugs or filenames.
